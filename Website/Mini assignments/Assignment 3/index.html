Flash Warning

  • Generative Motion

This piece can be very annoying to the human eye, which is why I named it Flash Warning. The object's motions are generative, and are iin continuos motion, which is the concept of this work. There is oscillation and linear motion involved in the creation of these object's movements as one can notice.
